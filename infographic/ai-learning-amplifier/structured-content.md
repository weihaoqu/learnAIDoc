# The Right Way: AI as Learning Amplifier

## Overview
A framework showing how to use AI effectively for learning: keep your brain engaged, think first, then leverage AI in three specific roles — tutor, challenger, and connector.

## Learning Objectives
The viewer will understand:
1. Your brain must stay "always on" when using AI to learn
2. The correct flow: Think first → Form questions → Attempt it
3. Three specific AI roles that amplify learning (tutor, challenger, connector)
4. AI extends your reach — you stay in control

## Design Instructions

### Style Preferences
- Deep indigo (#1E1B4B) background matching the article cover
- Electric purple (#8B5CF6) for brain/thinking elements
- Neon cyan (#06B6D4) for AI elements
- Amber (#F59E0B) for connections and highlights
- Neon glow effects on all nodes and connecting lines
- Dark atmospheric background with subtle circuit patterns

### Layout Preferences
- Tree-branching: brain at top, three branches flowing downward
- Clear connecting lines with glow effects
- Each branch visually distinct but equally weighted
- Tagline at bottom as grounding element

### Other Requirements
- Landscape (16:9) aspect ratio
- Will be embedded in a wiki article — should be clear and readable at medium size
- Match the aesthetic of the cover image (vivid flat-vector on dark background)
